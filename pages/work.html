about

Nicole is a medical student rooted in New Jersey and homed in Providence, Rhode Island. She (lovingly) holds a fascination with image-guided procedures and a passion for exploring the use of multimedia in education and scientific communication.

When not in the hospital / fulfilling student duties, Nicole can be found tinkering with mirrorless cameras, aiming for fairways, or immersed in (fantastical, tabletop) gameplay.
